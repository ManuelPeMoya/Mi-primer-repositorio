Coffee
Coffee is a brewed beverage with a distinct aroma and flavor prepared from the
roasted seeds of the Coffea plant.
Tea
Tea is an aromatic beverage commonly prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over
cured leaves of the tea plant.
Hot chocolate
Hot chocolate (also known as hot cocoa) is a heated beverage typically consisting of
shaved chocolate, melted chocolate or cocoa powder, heated milk or water, and sugar.
