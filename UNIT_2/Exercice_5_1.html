Building MSF

Since 1980, MSF has opened offices in 28 countries and employs more than 30,000
people across the world. Since its founding, MSF has treated over a hundred million
patients.

MSF has also maintained itsinstitutional and financial independence, and the
organization has continued to be critical of both itself and the broader aid system
when appropriate, all in the name of trying to help direct more effective and timely
aid to those who need it most.
